Fetches your Community Stats + Votes from all of your post.

Before you ask why does it not show all of my work in there? Have you reacted to your own work? If you didn't, do that first and then you should be able to see one there.

If a page isn't provided, it defaults to the number 1, if you pass a number from the page argument, it'll go to that specific page, however if you pass an invalid number, it defaults to 1 instead.

## Arguments:
`.cmStats (page)`

## Example Usage:
```
> .cmStats
>> Page stuff goes here...
```
